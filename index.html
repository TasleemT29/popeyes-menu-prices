Popeyes Menu Prices & Deals Guide

Save Big on Your Next Meal

Popeyes is famous for its crispy fried chicken, tasty sides, and buttery biscuits. But what makes it even better? Getting your meal at a great price! Whether you’re craving a Chicken Sandwich, Family Meal, or the popular Tuesday Special, there are many ways to save.

Save with Combo Meals

One of the best ways to save money is by choosing Popeyes combo meals. These usually include a main dish, side, and drink — all for a lower price than buying each item separately. The Chicken Sandwich Combo or the 2-Piece Chicken Combo are both filling and budget-friendly.

Family Meals for Bigger Savings

If you’re feeding the family, Popeyes’ Family Meals are a great option. These meals come with plenty of chicken, sides, and biscuits — perfect for sharing without spending too much.

Don’t Miss the Tuesday Special

The Tuesday Special is a fan favorite and a must-try deal. Every Tuesday, you can grab a 2-piece chicken (usually a leg and a thigh) for a lower price than normal. It’s a simple and affordable way to enjoy your Popeyes favorites.

Get More with Rewards and App Deals

Don’t forget to check out the Popeyes rewards program or app deals. You might find discounts, free sides, or even bonus chicken. Pairing these with daily specials gives you even more for your money.

Whether you’re grabbing a quick bite or feeding the whole family, Popeyes' menu and deals make it easy to enjoy a tasty meal without spending too much.
